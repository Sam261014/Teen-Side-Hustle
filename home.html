Ways to earn money by doing sidehustle.

1. Tutoring

  • Subject Tutoring:Help your peers in subject you excel in, like math, sciences, or individuals and societies through in-person lessons.
  • Language Tutoring: Teach a language you're fluent in to beginners or intermediate learners.
  • Online Tutoring: Use online tutoring platforms to teach various subjects you're proficient in, on platforms like Tutorpeers.

2. Flexible Jobs

  • Freelancing:Offer services like writing, graphic design, video editing, or creating a website on platforms like Fiverr and Upwork.
  • Pet Services: Offer pet sitting, or dog walking by starting with asking friends, family or neighbors. Platforms like Rover are also a great option for looking jobs about pet-sitting.
  • Household Tasks Provide babysitting or cleaning jobs in your community

3. Selling Products and Services

  • E-commerce: Make and Sell handmade crafts such as jewelry, paintings, or digital artwork on Etsy. You can also dropship products on platforms like eBay, Facebook Marketplace.
  • Affiliate Marketing: Promote products or services by advertising and earn commissions through affiliate programs.

4. Passive Income options

  • Investing: Build an emergency fund and invest in stocks, bonds, or mutual funds for passive income. Some great platforms to learn about how on invest as a teeanger include Teenvestor
  • Content Creation: Start a YouTube channel, TikTok, or blog and monetize through ads, sponsorships, or affiliate marketing.

5. Utilizing Your Skills and Hobbies

  • Stock Photography:Sell your photos on stock websites like Shutterstock or Adobe Stock.
  • Sports refreeing Become a referee for sports like football or basketball. Get certified and earn money refreeing games in your community or local leagues.
  • Music and Arts: Offer music lessons, art classes, or perform at events.

6. Participating in Research and Surveys

  • Online Surveys and Studies: Participate in online surveys or research studies for small earnings.
  • User Testing: Test websites and apps for usability and provide feedback.

7. Educational and Skill Development

  • Online Courses: Create and sell online courses on platforms like Udemy or Teachable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need parental permission to start earning money?

A: It depends on the type of work. Always consult your parents or guardians before starting a new project.

Q: How much time should I dedicate to earning money?

A: Balance is key. It is a must to ensure that your studies and well-being are your top priorities while setting aside specific hours for your project.

Q: Are there any risks involved?

A: Always research platforms and clients thoroughly. Avoid sharing sensitive personal information and stay cautious of scams.
